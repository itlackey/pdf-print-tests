Verification Checklist

When comparing PagedJS and Vivliostyle output, verify:

  1. All pages render correctly
  2. Page breaks occur at expected locations
  3. Running headers display chapter titles
  4. Page numbers are correct
  5. Footnotes appear at page bottom
  6. Floats position correctly
  7. Writing modes display properly
  8. All visual effects render
